Why Epicodus? Two words: Work. Remote.

I've spent the past 8 years working in ...non-standard... occupations; like guiding climbers up big walls, training scuba-divers in Thailand, or leading troops in the military. It's been a great adventure, but now I'm looking for a career change that will let me do those things for FUN, rather than for work!

Programming has always seemed appealing, but intimidating. WHile going through my quarter-life-crisis, I've been looking for a stimulating career change that wouldn't require an additional 4 years of university. Having seen a few friends go through similar bootcamps, it sounded like a great option!

Having a blast so far, and can't wait to really dive in and learn to build some awesome things!
